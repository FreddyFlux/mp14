FAQ

What is included?

We take care of including a complete program from arrival to departure of the camps. We serve breakfast in the morning in good time before practice and accomodate food that the participants will bring to their field practice. After a day of practice we serve a joint dinner in the facilities.

What do I need to prepare before going on the camp?

You need to bring a bag that you can bring to practice, a water bottle and indoor shoes. You should also have change for the ammount of days there is training.

Can you arrange so a group of friends can share accomodation?

Send us the information on post@mp-14.com and we will do our best to accomodate your request.

Is it possible to cancel the booked camp?

You can cancel the booking latest 1 month before end-date of booking.

Anything else?

Fill out your name and email and send us a message so we can cater to your needs!
